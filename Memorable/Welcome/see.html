About Memorable

Memorable was created as a learning project by Anirudh Iyer. The intent was merely to improve proficiency in programming. However, as the project developed, several real usecases were discovered and... Memorable was born.

Starting with the simplistic user interface and seamless note-taking experience, to dark mode and AI integration, Memorable is fully equipped for all your needs.

Are you a vampire like me and can't stand light mode? Switch to our cool dark mode with just one press.

At a loss for ideas? No problem. With Memorable's integrated AI, we offer all the encouragement you can possibly need.

Wish to check up on some facts? No need to open google. Access with yet another handy button.
